    Lake Land'Or Classic

    Welcome to the Lake Land'Or Classic, the most anticipated golf event of the year! Nestled in the scenic beauty of Lake Land'Or, this tournament is more than just a competition—it's a reunion of old friends, a celebration of camaraderie, and a weekend of unforgettable memories. Whether you're a seasoned golfer or a casual player, the Lake Land'Or Classic promises an exciting and relaxed atmosphere where the focus is on fun. Expect plenty of laughs, spirited competition, and maybe even a bit of good-natured trash-talking. It's all about getting rowdy with friends, both old and new, on and off the greens. Join us for a weekend filled with golf, great food, cold drinks, and lively entertainment. The Lake Land'Or Classic is where friendships are rekindled, and new ones are forged, all in the name of the game we love. Mark your calendars and get ready to tee off at the Lake Land'Or Classic—where the golf is great, but the company is even better!
